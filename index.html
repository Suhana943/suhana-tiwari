Project: Smart Home Energy Monitoring System with Predictive Analytics

Role: IoT Developer

Solution & Results: Successfully deployed a prototype system that provided accurate real-time energy data. Users of the prototype reported an average 10-15% reduction in energy consumption due to actionable insights.
